About the EcoFuelizer

The EcoFuelizer uses cutting-edge pyrolysis technology to convert household plastic waste into gasoline and diesel. It's eco-friendly, solar-powered, and designed to help families and small businesses lower their energy costs while reducing plastic waste.

Project Details

The EcoFuelizer is designed to help reduce plastic waste while generating fuel. Below are the details of the project:

Process Overview

EcoFuelizer uses pyrolysis to heat plastic to high temperatures (400-500°C) in the absence of oxygen, turning plastic into fuel vapors. These vapors are then condensed into liquid fuels like gasoline and diesel.

What is Pyrolysis?

Pyrolysis is a thermochemical process that decomposes organic materials at elevated temperatures in the absence of oxygen. During pyrolysis, long-chain polymers in plastics break down into smaller hydrocarbons, which are vaporized and then condensed into liquid fuels.

Pyrolysis Stages

  • Heating the Plastic: The plastic waste is heated in a chamber with no oxygen, reaching temperatures of up to 500°C.
  • Decomposition: The high heat causes the plastic to decompose into gases, including methane, propane, and butane, as well as longer-chain hydrocarbons.
  • Condensation: These gases are cooled down, condensing into liquid forms like gasoline and diesel.

Estimated Output

  • For every 5 kg of plastic waste, the EcoFuelizer produces:
  • 2.5 liters of gasoline
  • 0.5 liters of diesel

Energy Consumption

  • Heating Element Power: 1.5 kW
  • Total Energy Consumption per Batch: 7.5 kWh
  • Cost of Electricity: $0.12/kWh

Earnings Calculation

  • Electricity Cost per Batch: $0.90
  • Fuel Value from 5 kg of Plastic:
  • 2.5 liters of gasoline at $0.925/liter = $2.31
  • 0.5 liters of diesel at $1.057/liter = $0.53
  • Total Fuel Value per Batch: $2.84

Net Profit Calculation

  • Net Profit per Batch: $2.84 - $0.95 = $1.89
  • Time to Earn Back Initial Investment ($1,000): 530 batches (~1.45 years)

Environmental Impact

Using the EcoFuelizer, a typical family can convert their plastic waste into fuel every 3-4 days, drastically reducing their contribution to plastic pollution. The EcoFuelizer also lowers the need for conventional fuels, reducing overall carbon footprints.
